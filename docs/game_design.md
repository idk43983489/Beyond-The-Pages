# Game Design

## Core Idea

Beyond The Pages is a basketball universe simulator focused on creating stories, rivalries, relationships, and history beyond statistics.

The goal is to create a living basketball world where players, teams, coaches, and franchises develop their own stories over time.

## Design Pillars

### 1. Players are individuals, not ratings
Every player should have unique personalities, relationships, goals, and stories.

### 2. Relationships matter
Rivalries, friendships, conflicts, and chemistry should affect the basketball world.

### 3. History matters
Past events should influence the future through records, legacies, and narratives.

### 4. Decisions have consequences
Trades, contracts, coaching decisions, and team choices should create long-term effects.

### 5. Every save creates a unique story
No two basketball universes should develop exactly the same way.

## Main Systems

### League System
Controls teams, schedules, standings, playoffs, and league history.

### Player System
Controls player creation, ratings, attributes, personalities, careers, and development.

### Simulation System
Controls how games are simulated, including player performance, team strategy, and results.

### Relationship System
Controls rivalries, friendships, chemistry, conflicts, and player interactions.

### Transaction System
Controls trades, free agency, contracts, drafts, and team building.

### Story System
Creates events, news, press conferences, and moments that shape league history.
